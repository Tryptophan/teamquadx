FLL and FTC Mentoring

We aim to spread FIRST throughout the community, but we also feel its important to give guidance and mentor those who are already involved! To accomplish this, we made it a priority to be someone who all our local FLL and even other FTC teams can look to for advice and help! We do this by holding "Saturday Build Days" where we invite all the local FTC teams to come out and continue building and improving their skills for the upcoming competitions! We provide parts, computers, resources for software and marketing, mentors, arenas, and even a 3D printer for any custom parts that they might need! We also go out on a weekly basis and provide mentoring and help to our local FLL teams, including "FalTech" and "Fear The Gear". Through this we have developed a close relationship that we hope will encourage them to continue FIRST through their middle school, and eventually high school years.

Community Outreach

We realized how lucky we all are to live in such a comfortable life, and how much we wanted to bring the amazing opportunities that FIRST presents to others too. To help spread STEM, we decided to focus on helping kids with their academics. We go out and tutor kids from south Austin on a weekly basis. We help them with any subject they are struggling on, and also encourage them to think about their future in STEM and FTC.
