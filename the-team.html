Team Bonding

To succeed in the game this year, we decided that it was imperative for us to all become closer! We built ourselves as a tight-knit group of friends, and because of this, we formed a seamless, hard-working team. We all dedicate a lot to time to Robotics and FTC, and as a result, we are together more often than we aren’t! To help alleviate the stress that comes with competition, high school, and general life, planning fun activities becomes a must! We joined robotics to do what we love, but having you team also be the people who you will make life-long friendships with is what FTC is all about.
