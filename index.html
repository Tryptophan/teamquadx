Our Mission

As a team of well-rounded students from Vandegrift High School, our goal is to learn the aspects of building and programming a successful robot optimized for competition. We make an effort to embody the different fields of STEM in order to develop a better understanding on our task and to apply our skills in the real world. Our team aims to showcase our robot and bring unmatched spirit to the world of Robotics. We believe in the true spirit of competition: competing in order to learn and better ourselves. Embodying the spirit of FIRST Robotics, we strive endlessly to learn, help others, and, ultimately, have fun.

For our team, gracious professionalism means so much more than simply helping out a team in need. It means empathy for others, friendly competition, and living with the true spirit of learning. We hope to provide a good example of sportsmanship for other teams as well, both in and out of competition.
