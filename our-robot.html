Our Robot

Our robot is built for speed reliability and durability. We have learned through our years of experience that a robot must be extremely reliable to be competitive, our robot is built to score the same amount every time with very little variability. We have also learned speed is key a fast robot will beat a slower robot anyday . Our last principle is durability, when building our robot we focused on including redundant systems and hope wiring to minimize the possibility of something going wrong during the game.

Linear Slides

We use 80/20 for our lift, because it makes a much smoother linear slide than other materials we have used in the past. It also give us much better structural integrity and will be able to withstand a lot more abuse in the field.

5 Inch Wheels

We custom developed and printed specialized 5in wheels over the summer, before you could buy them off the shelves. Having these 5in wheels directly mounted to the motors greatly increases our speed without having to use unreliable high maintenance mechanisms like chain drives.
